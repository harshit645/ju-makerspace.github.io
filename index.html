Welcome to JU MAKERSPACE

What is JU MAKERSPACE ?

"Making is fundamental to what it means to be human.” JU MAKERSPACE is a collaborative work space in JECRC University, Jaipur aiming to foster projects at the intersection of technology, creativity and innovation. It is about an experience not expertise. It allows everyone to ideate, experiment, prototype, and build their ideas. The goal is to work together to learn, collaborate, and share and aalso to combine DIY with education. It’s all about designing and thinking!
