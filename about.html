We have successfully done many events like : On 20 Dec JU MAKERSPACE conducted a Faculty Workshop on the same which highlighted the key features of 3-D printing and designing the CAD model. Our Mission

“A MakerSpace is a metaphor for a unique learning environment that encourages tinkering, play and open-ended exploration for all.” We @ JU MAKERSPACE provide you opportunity to engage in new experiences, develop your skills, and ignite ambition. By being a part of our community, not only you will enhance your learning experience but also expose yourself to new opportunities in your future. You can work with us on the following skills-

 Coding
 3d printing
 Soldering
 Electronics / Arduino
 Wood working

JU MakerSpace facilitates experimentation and failure, builds career and life skills, and inspires new connections and creative explorations.

What is SW-MKS?

SW-MKS

SW-MKS (Summer with MakerSpace) is an free workshop provided by members of JU MakerSpace to motivate students to explore their areas of interest in some of the most trending technologies. These series of structured sessions will provide participants with knowledge and skills related to the new technology. SW-MKS is going to be an enriching session to help develop the students’ interest in the engineering world in a fun and interactive way.


Benefits Of SW-MKS

 The workshop will provide you with ideas on Python, Machine Learning, Arduino, Web Development, Control Algorithms for Drones and much more.
 This will help you develop your interest in trending technology fields.
 It can act as a kick start to your learning if you are struck in between.
 You will learn some new and important skills which will make you shine brightly among the ordinary.
